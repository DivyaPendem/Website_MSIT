MSIT - Master of Science in Information Technology

MSIT is a 2 year intensive postgraduate degree program for all engineering (B.E. and B.Tech) graduates aspiring to be IT professionals.

MSIT curriculum is designed with the fundamentals of computer science, soft skills and specializations in Software Engineering, Computer Networks, Data Analytics and Visualization, Information Secuirty and eBusiness Technologies.

A major chunk of the students' workload at MSIT is devoted to "deliberate practice" through Learn by Doing projects. A successful MSIT graduate, by the end of the 2 years, demonstrates mastery level competence in computational thinking and problem solving.

In the last 14 years over 1500 MSITians have shaped into stunning careers in computer science, IT and several other IT enabled domains. The well rounded MSIT curriculum with a world class learning environment prepares students to work in leading multinationals.

Consortium of Universities

A consortium of universities in collaboration with Carnegie Mellon University offers the MSIT program.

IIIT Hyderabad, JNTU Hyderabad, JNTU Kakinada and JNTU Ananthapur are part of the consoritum. Students are admitted to one of the 4 universities based on the order of merit.

University to which the student is admitted to awards the MSIT degree upon the completion of the graduation requirements.

Learning by Doing

"I hear I forget.
 I see and I remember.
 I do and I understand."
- Confucius

MSIT courses are designed based on this philosophy. Every course features a set of compelling learn by doing projects.

Students learn the concepts and then attempt the projects. The projects give ample opportunity for learning and practice.

Experience on projects boots students confidence.

Mentor Model

Every student has unique capabilities. MSIT learn by doing projects are a great way for the students to showcase their talent to the world.

Our 1:10 mentor to student ratio gives our mentors an opportunity to work with their students in a personalized manner.

Students are given projects and they are stuck. Mentors recover them by giving examples and guide them to finish. During the project students learn a lot from the mistakes they make and from the mentor's feedback.

Mentors take on the role as guardians to the students. They constantly motivate them, keep them on track and help them learning better.

IT Core Curriculum

To graduate every students needs 160 IT credits and 40 Soft Skills credits.

There are several programming courses begining with an Introduction to Programming, Object Oriented Programming, 3 courses on Algorithms and Data Structures and a continuous programming course.

Students learn Databases, Web Development, Software Engineering, Operating Systems and Computer Networks to finish the first year course load.

Specialization Tracks

Specialization track gives students an opportunity to learn a domain in depth. Each track has 4 courses and a 6 months capstone project. In the second year of MSIT, students choose from one of the following specialization tracks.

  1. Software Engineering
  2. Data Analytics and Visualization
  3. Mobile and Web App Development
  4. Computer Networks
  5. Information Security

Emerging technologies are reviewed every year to create new and in demand specialization tracks.
